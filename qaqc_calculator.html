How to use this tool

1. To Estimate Time: Enter the Total Project Design Hours in the box below and click "Calculate Review Time". This determines the Project Size (S, M, L, etc.) and expected duration.
2. To Calculate Grade: After entering hours, expand the "Calculate Project Score" section. Select the OpCo/Voltage to check TPP, enter your error counts, and click Calculate.
3. To Find Deadlines: First complete Step 1 to set the duration. Then expand the "Date Calculators" section to determine when to submit or when you'll get it back.
